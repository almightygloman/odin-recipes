Haggis

Description

Prepare your taste buds for a daring expedition into the realm of haggis, Scotland's "delicacy" that defies all notions of culinary sanity. Picture this: a mishmash of sheep's innards awkwardly intertwined with onions, oatmeal, and a generous serving of suet – because who doesn't love fatty surprises! Topped off with the pièce de résistance, the stomach lining of a sheep (because apparently, plates are overrated).

Ingredients

  • Sheep's heart, liver, and lungs (traditionally minced)
  • Onions
  • Oatmeal
  • Suet (beef or lamb fat)
  • Stock (usually lamb or beef)
  • Salt and pepper
  • Spices (such as nutmeg and cloves)

Steps

  1. Throw it all together
  2. Go to the dentist
  3. Oy oy mate its chewsday innit
  4. Bukayo Saka is overrated
